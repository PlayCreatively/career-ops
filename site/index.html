Each row is a group — a named bucket of filters matched against one field (role, studio, location…). Click the group name to rename it, change its field, or delete it; the button by it combines its matched ratings — max (best wins) · min (worst wins) · avg. Every filter is a rating that orders “Best match” — it widens, it doesn't restrict. Click a filter to cycle inactive ▸ on (+) ▸ filter-out; tap the ✎ to edit its name, keyword list, and a fine-grained 0–2 weight (− at 0 hides matches, ~ below 1 lowers, + 1 is on / above boosts). A catch-all filter matches when nothing else in its group does.
