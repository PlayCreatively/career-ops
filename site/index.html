Each row is a group — a named bucket of filters matched against one field (role, studio, location…). Click the group name to rename it, change its field, or delete it; the button by it combines its matched ratings — max (best wins) · min (worst wins) · avg. Every filter is a rating that feeds the Rating column — sort by it (or any header) to order the table. Click a filter to cycle inactive ▸ on (+) ▸ filter-out. Each pill shows a view⁄total count — how many of its matches are in the current list vs. in total; click either number to isolate the list to just that set (left = within your other filters, right = all of them). Tap 👁 to mute a filter momentarily (ignore it without losing its weight); tap the ✎ to edit its name, keyword list, and a fine-grained 0–2 weight (− at 0 hides matches, ~ below 1 lowers, + 1 is on / above boosts). A catch-all filter matches when nothing else in its group does.
